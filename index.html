Who are We?

What began as a small family dream soon took flight, literally, when we won the lottery. We bought our first rocketship that year. The goal in mind? Make space travel accessible for all! Since then we have grown a fleet of 5 rocketships and plan to increase further.

We can take you there! with affordable prices, and payment plans available, you too can see our galaxy and beyond!

Visit the Moon

Our most affordable pricing plan. The moon tour will take you and your family to the moon and back, literally. Our space stations on the moon have rest rooms, an observatory, and many places to eat. Also available on this plan is the option of a moon walk, where you will be able to experience the feeling of anti-gravity. How exciting! Plans start at $12,000 and can be paid up over several years.
